# 🗓️ Day 17 — Edge & eBPF

Today you'll get a preview of advanced networking — load balancers, CDNs, firewalls, and the eBPF technology that's reshaping cloud-native networking.

## Key Learnings

- Firewalls: iptables/nftables basics
- eBPF: what it is and why it matters for networking
- `iptables` / `nft` — packet filtering
